## Criminal Organization Overview

### **ACTIVELY HUNTED (Kill-on-Sight):**

**1. Veinshadow Covenant**
- **Crime:** Bloodline theft and forced transplantation via torture
- **Why Hunted:** Violates fundamental cultivation ethics
- **Danger:** HIGH to anyone with Noble+ bloodlines
- **Methods:** Organized hunter squads, tactical captures
- **Recent Activity:** Desperation-driven expansion since invasion

**3. Corrupted Devil Cults**
- **Crime:** Sin Cultivation, demon worship, blood sacrifice
- **Why Hunted:** Direct servants of the Seven Generals, spread corruption
- **Danger:** EXTREME - triple power boost from Sin techniques
- **Methods:** Secret cells, kidnapping, forced conversion, intelligence gathering
- **Recent Activity:** Networks spread globally in 5 years

### **TOLERATED (Necessary Evil):**

**2. Rogue Cultivator Guilds**
- **Service:** Mercenary combat, protection, assassination
- **Why Tolerated:** Alliance needs them for tasks sects won't do
- **Danger:** Variable - professional but honor-bound to contracts
- **Methods:** Contract-based work, no ideological alignment
- **Moral Issue:** Some take contracts from BOTH orthodox and cultists

**4. Shadowmarket Guild**
- **Service:** Forbidden goods, illegal resources, information brokering
- **Why Tolerated:** Too embedded to eliminate, both sides secretly use
- **Danger:** LOW combat (hire others), HIGH influence
- **Methods:** Black market sales, intelligence trading, smuggling
- **Moral Issue:** Sells to demons and orthodox alike, pure profit motive

## Character Hooks by Organization

**Character Types:**
- Anyone with Noble/Ancient/Divine/Primordial bloodline (HIGH RISK)
- Bloodline theft survivors seeking revenge
- Orthodox cultivators hunting Covenant members
- Imperial Corps investigators tracking family clans
- Cultivators approached by Covenant for recruitment (moral choice)

**Story Hooks:**
- Rescue kidnapped bloodline victim
- Infiltrate Covenant clan to gather intelligence
- Survive Covenant hunter squad ambush
- Moral dilemma: Accept demon power to fight Covenant?
- Family member disappeared—Covenant suspected

### Rogue Cultivator Guilds - Moral Complexity

**Character Types:**
- Expelled sect disciples joining guilds

- Mercenaries questioning their contracts
