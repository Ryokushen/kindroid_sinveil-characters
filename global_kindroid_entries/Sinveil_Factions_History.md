**Keywords:** `Sin Legions` `Seven Generals` `Vaelen` `Pride` `Greed` `Wrath` `devils`

```
Seven Sin Legions serve seven Generals who escaped the Abyss 5 years ago through Continental Gates. Generals: Vaelen (Pride), Aurex (Greed), Vespera (Temptation), Elowen (Envy), Glut (Gluttony), Calderon (Wrath), Morvane (Sloth). Each commands corrupted cultivators plus native devils. ## Journal Entry 5: The Great Rift Opening
```
**Keywords:** `Great Rift` `5 years ago` `Continental Gates` `invasion` `Abyss`

```
Five years ago, the Seven Generals breached the Veil after 3,000 years sealed in the Abyss. Opened 8 Continental Gates across five continents. No Sovereign defenders remained—King Titan died 20 years prior. Generals chose covert strategy: corruption over conquest. Orthodox Alliance remains divided between Believers (mobilizing), Investigators (gathering intelligence), and Skeptics (dismissing threat). Gates remain open; portals form more easily with fractured Veil.
```
**Characters:** 486

## Journal Entry 6: Five Continents of Sinveil
**Keywords:** `continents` `Solhara` `Frostholm` `Mistmere` `Primereach` `Scattered Isles`

```
Sinveil has five continents. Solhara: sun-scorched deserts/tropics, home to Blazing Heart Sect (Fire). Frostholm: mountain spine/tundra, home to Unshaken Foundation (Earth) and Iron Body (Body). Mistmere: veiled marshes/fog, home to Veiled Mercy (Shadow). Primereach: balanced temperate heartland, most populous, hosts Alliance HQ Ebonspire, home to Righteous Blade (Sword) and Tranquil Depths (Water). Scattered Isles: broken archipelago, home to Soaring Freedom (Wind) and Eternal Dawn (Light).
```
