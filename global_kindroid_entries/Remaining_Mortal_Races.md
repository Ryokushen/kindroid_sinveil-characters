## Journal Entry 1: Lepori Race
**Keywords:** `lepori` `bunnyfolk` `rabbit` `scouts` `messengers` `Wind affinity`

```
Lepori are bunnyfolk built for speed and evasion. Large ears (enhanced hearing), powerful legs for jumping and kicks, fragile build. Wind/Light Paths: 0% penalty; others -50%. "Flight over fight" philosophy—extreme evasion specialists, kite and strike from safety. Serve as scouts and messengers. Natural danger sense, exceptional speed and jumping. Wind cultivators = fastest messengers/scouts in Sinveil. Light cultivators make excellent healers. Prefer bows, throwing weapons, ranged combat. Family protection over personal glory. Rarely stand and fight.
```

## Journal Entry 2: Orc Race
**Keywords:** `orc` `orcs` `orcish` `tusks` `berserker` `warrior culture`

```

Enhanced strength, pain tolerance, natural regeneration. Berserker rage potential when injured/angry (power boost). Tusks for piercing, enhanced unarmed strikes. Fire cultivators = berserker pyromancers growing stronger in battle. Body cultivators = nearly unkillable juggernauts. Earth cultivators = living walls armies break against. Prefer axes, greatswords, warhammers.

## Journal Entry 3: Goblin Race
**Keywords:** `goblin` `goblins` `cunning` `trappers` `Shadow affinity` `ambush`

```
Goblins are small, nimble opportunists with sharp teeth. Shadow/Wind Paths: 0% penalty; others -50%. Cunning and adaptable survival instincts. Small size (harder to hit, fit tight spaces), enhanced dexterity, excellent reflexes. Natural scavengers finding resources others miss. Opportunistic ambushers—attack weakness, flee when threatened. Excel at guerrilla tactics and hit-and-run. Shadow cultivators = exceptional trappers/infiltrators. Wind cultivators = nearly impossible to catch. High-realm Goblins terrifyingly effective despite being "weak." Prefer daggers, crossbows, traps.
```

## Journal Entry 4: Harpy Race
**Keywords:** `harpy` `harpies` `wings` `flight` `talons` `aerial combat` `Wind affinity`

```
Harpies are winged humanoids with natural flight from birth. Wind/Light Paths: 0% penalty; others -45%. Wings, talons for slashing, beak for piercing, hollow bones (lighter but fragile), enhanced vision (see incredible distances). Aerial superiority fighters—strike from above, stay out of reach. Harpy songs can disorient or inspire. Vulnerable in prolonged ground combat. Wind cultivators = unmatched aerial combatants (3D combat mastery). Light cultivators "sing dawn into existence." Prefer spears, javelins, throwing weapons usable in flight.

## Journal Entry 5: Centaur Race
**Keywords:** `centaur` `centaurs` `four legs` `hooves` `charging` `cavalry`

```
Centaurs are four-legged warriors (horse lower body, human upper body). Wind/Earth/Body Paths: 0% penalty; others -45%. Nomadic warrior culture valuing speed and charging power. Extreme speed and charging momentum, four legs provide incredible stability, powerful hoof kicks. Can fight while running. Charge-based warriors using mass and momentum. Vulnerable in confined spaces. Wind cultivators = unstoppable cavalry. Earth cultivators = living siege engines. Body cultivators = legendary endurance (run for days while fighting). Prefer spears, bows, lances usable while running.
```

```

Earth cultivators = living fortress-breakers. Body cultivators can headbutt through steel. Fire cultivators = berserker infernos. Prefer greataxes, massive warhammers. ## Journal Entry 8: Nereid Race
**Keywords:** `nereid` `nereids` `water folk` `aquatic` `gills` `bioluminescent` `Water affinity`

```
Nereids are aquatic humanoids, THE definitive Water race. Height 160-190cm, lifespan 400-600 years. Skin: pale blue to deep teal, webbed fingers/toes, gill slits, bioluminescent markings glowing with qi. Water/Light Paths: 0% penalty; others -45%. Perfect underwater combat (no penalties), breathe water and air equally, pressure resistance (deep ocean), enhanced swimming. Fluid adaptive combat, weaponize surrounding water. Light cultivators combine bioluminescence + healing. Strong presence in Tranquil Depths Sect. Famous Tidekeeper Guard patrols Alliance coastlines. Prefer tridents, spears, nets.
```
