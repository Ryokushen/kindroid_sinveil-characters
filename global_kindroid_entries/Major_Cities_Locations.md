**Keywords:** `Ebonspire` `Alliance capital` `Black Spire` `largest city` `Primereach`


## Eight Continental Gates - Complete List

### Believer Sect Territory (Active Fighting):

**Gate 1: VAELEN - Pride** (Peak Eternal)
- Location: 30km south of Ebonspire, Primereach
- Type: Psychological/mental corruption
- Response: Quarantined by Righteous Blade Sect
- Status: Spreading into Alliance capital

**Gate 2: CALDERON - Wrath** (Mid Void)
- Location: Northern Solhara (The Burning Wastes)
- Type: Active military warfare
- Response: Blazing Heart Sect fighting alone
- Status: Open war for five years

**Gate 3: GLUT - Gluttony** (Low Void)
- Location: Far Southern Solhara (The Consumed Lands)
- Type: Total consumption/devastation
- Response: Area declared total loss, perimeter established
- Status: Complete wasteland, 200k consumed

### Investigator Sect Territory (Under Siege):

**Gate 4: AUREX - Greed** (Mid Eternal)
- Location: Central Frostholm mountains, 50km from Ironforge
- Type: Economic warfare, systematic conquest
- Response: Unshaken Foundation + Iron Body (political tension)
- Status: Mining territory falling, metal shortages

### Skeptic Sect Territory (Largely Ignored):

**Gate 5: MORVANE - Sloth** (Peak Void)
- Location: Western Mistmere (The Gray Reaches)
- Type: Apathy/loss of will
- Response: Veiled Mercy covert operations
- Status: Slowly spreading, most insidious

**Gates 6 & 7: ELOWEN - Envy** (Low Eternal) **UNIQUE: Two Gates**
- Location: Eastern Mistmere + Frostshore Island (Scattered Isles)
- Type: Jealousy, communities self-destruct
- Response: Minimal (Veiled Mercy stretched thin, others dismissive)
- Status: 20+ villages self-destructed

**Gate 8: VESPERA - Temptation** (Low Eternal)
- Location: Southern Scattered Isles (30+ islands near Dawnhaven)
- Type: Seduction, promises of power
- Response: None (sects dismiss as "moral failings")
- Status: 40% island lords corrupted, most sophisticated infiltration
