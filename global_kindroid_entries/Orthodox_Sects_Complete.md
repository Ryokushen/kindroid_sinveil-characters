## Character Creation Hooks

### Fire Path (Blazing Heart Sect)
- **Passionate warrior** frustrated with political hesitation
- **Dragonkin** seeking to prove courage
- **Fire-drake rider** patrolling borders
- Character who **wants immediate action** against demons

### Earth Path (Unshaken Foundation Sect)
- **Cautious investigator** gathering evidence
- **Dwarf** with connections to missing mining towns
- **Protector** preparing defenses quietly
- Character who **knew King Titan** or studies his legacy

### Water Path (Tranquil Depths Sect)
- **Scholar** researching First Sin War
- **Diplomat** trying to unite Alliance
- **Librarian** who discovered forbidden knowledge
- Character with **access to ancient secrets**

### Wind Path (Soaring Freedom Sect)
- **Sky-ship merchant** encountering demons but leadership won't listen
- **Free spirit** who values liberty over security
- **Traveler** who's seen the truth firsthand
- Character facing **choice between freedom and duty**

### Light Path (Eternal Dawn Sect)
- **Healer** treating refugees without asking why they flee
- **Naive optimist** learning harsh truths
- **Purification specialist** (like Elder Serina) who sees the threat
- Character near **Vespera's portal** in danger
