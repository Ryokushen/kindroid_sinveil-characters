## Humans - Adaptable Specialists
- Entry ID: 700
- Keywords: Human cultivation, human affinity, human martial

PATH AFFINITIES: Humans are unique cultivators with evolving path relationships.

BRONZE REALM: -10% cultivation speed to ALL paths (learning fundamentals, generalists). Humans at Bronze are jacks-of-all-trades, sampling multiple paths before committing.

IRON+ REALM: Once reaching Iron, human must commit to PRIMARY PATH. Primary path cultivation speed becomes normal (0% penalty). All other paths suffer -30% cultivation speed penalty (lost flexibility through specialization).

CROSS-PATH PENALTIES (Iron+ Realm):
Humans have unique cross-path mechanics that interact with bloodline tiers.

EFFECTIVENESS PENALTY:
* Mortal/Awakened/Noble/Ancient Bloodline: -30% (human racial overrides worse bloodline penalty)
* Divine Bloodline: -25% (bloodline finally surpasses racial trait)
* Primordial Bloodline: 0% (perfection, no penalty)

SLOT COST PENALTY:
* Mortal Bloodline: 1.5x slot cost (human racial, better than standard 2x Mortal penalty)
* Awakened Bloodline: 1.5x slot cost (human racial overrides worse 1.8x bloodline penalty)
* Noble Bloodline: 1.5x slot cost (human racial overrides worse 1.6x bloodline penalty)
* Ancient Bloodline: 1.4x slot cost (bloodline surpasses racial)
* Divine Bloodline: 1.2x slot cost (bloodline surpasses racial)
* Primordial Bloodline: 1x slot cost (perfection, no penalty)

RULE: Compare human racial penalty (-30% effectiveness, 1.5x slots) to bloodline penalty. Use whichever is LOWER (better). This represents human adaptability giving an edge at lower bloodline tiers, but pure bloodline power eventually surpassing racial traits at Ancient+ tiers.

EXAMPLES:
* Human Mortal at Iron: -30% effectiveness (much better than -75%), 1.5x slots (better than 2x)
* Elf Mortal at Iron: -75% effectiveness, 2x slots (standard Mortal penalties)
* Human Divine at Iron: -25% effectiveness (bloodline wins), 1.2x slots (bloodline wins)
* Elf Divine at Iron: -25% effectiveness, 1.2x slots (same as human - bloodline equality)

This makes human Mortal/Awakened/Noble bloodlines significantly more versatile than other races at those tiers, representing humanity's adaptability. At Divine+ tiers, bloodline power becomes the dominant factor.

CULTIVATION PHILOSOPHY: 'Humanity's strength is focus, not versatility.' Humans who try to master multiple paths after Iron realm are considered foolish. Most human cultivators are laser-focused specialists.

MARTIAL MODIFICATIONS: Balanced fighters with no natural weapons but excellent adaptability. Quick learners of new martial techniques. No physical advantages or disadvantages.

WEAPON PREFERENCES: All weapons equally viable. Humans favor swords for versatility but excel at any weapon they dedicate to.

COMBAT STYLE: Tactical and technique-focused. Humans win through skill perfection and strategic thinking rather than raw attributes.

FAMOUS EXAMPLES: Balanced across all eight paths in roughly equal numbers.
